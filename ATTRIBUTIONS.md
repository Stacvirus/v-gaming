# Asset Attributions

Before deploying V-GAMING to production, all 3D models and Lottie animations must be properly
attributed in accordance with their respective licenses. Failure to do so may constitute
copyright infringement.

## Important Notice

Do NOT use branded gaming controller icons, game franchise logos, or company trademarks without
an explicit commercial license from the rights holder. This includes PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo,
and other gaming brand assets. Always use CC0 or commercially-licensed originals.

## Recommended Sources for Free Commercial-Use Assets

- **Lottie animations**: https://lottiefiles.com (filter by "Free" license)
- **3D models (CC0)**: https://polyhaven.com/models
- **3D models (free)**: https://sketchfab.com/features/free-3d-models (check individual licenses)

## How to Update This File

When you replace a placeholder asset with a real one:
1. Add the source URL and author name to the table above
2. Confirm the license allows commercial use
3. If attribution is required by the license, add it to the website footer or a /credits page
